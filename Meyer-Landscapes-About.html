Meet Greg and Christine Meyer

Owners

Meyer Landscapes has been family owned and operated for over 15 years. Created in 2010 by Yarmouth residents Greg & Christine Meyer, the company became a proud member of the Cape Cod Landscape Association in 2012.

Greg is a graduate of the Massachusetts Stockbridge School of Agriculture, where he studied landscape design, contracting, and agriculture. Combining his education with over 25 years of work experience, Greg applies his extensive knowledge of the industry to every job that he works on.

Christine is a graduate of Suffolk University with a Master's in Business Administration. Co-owner and Office Manager at Meyer Landscapes, Christine is usually the first person you will speak with when you call. With over 15 years in the Accounting and Bookkeeping industry before joining her husband at Meyer Landscapes, she brings an extra level of expertise and customer communication to the company.

Greg and Christine were both born and raised on Cape Cod and have been married for nearly 15 years. They have a son together, who shares his father's passion for landscape equipment and “playing outside”.

Greg and Christine have spent the last decade establishing trusted relationships with their customers and within their community.

Meet Jesse Abate

Maintenance and Irrigation Manager

Jesse has been living and working on Cape Cod for over 20 years, and it's where he met his wife, Cate. A long-time employee of Meyer Landscapes, he manages the Irrigation and Maintenance Division. If you are a customer of any of our maintenance services, you have likely seen Jesse on the jobsite making sure that the project is running smoothly.

During the Winter months, Jesse manages the Home Watch Division, where he performs interior and exterior inspections. Prior to working in Landscaping, Jesse worked for three years as a Class 3 Engineer at a resort, where he was trained in Home Watch services. In addition to that, he was a firefighter for 4 years in Texas. Over the years, Jesse has worked closely with plumbers, electricians, and HVAC technicians; expanding his skills and knowledge in home maintenance and safety.

Whether you are a customer of Meyer Landscapes, or Meyer Home Watch, feel free to say “hello” to Jesse when you see him. He has a wealth of knowledge and always happy to answer any questions you have while he's there.

Meet Nick Eldredge

Hardscape Manager

Nick is a seasoned professional with over 15 years of experience in hardscapes and excavation. Born and raised in Harwich, Nick has a deep connection to the local community and its landscape.

For nearly seven years, Nick owned and operated a successful hardscape company, honing expertise in design, installation, and project management.

After building a strong foundation in the industry, Nick transitioned to Meyer Landscape, where he now leads the hardscape division, bringing his extensive knowledge and leadership to the team.

Nick currently resides in Dennis with his wife and two children.
