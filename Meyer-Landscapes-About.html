About Us

Meyer Landscapes has been family owned and operated for over 15 years. Created in 2010 by Yarmouth residents Greg & Christine Meyer, the company became a proud member of the Cape Cod Landscape Association in 2012. Greg is a graduate of the Massachusetts Stockbridge School of Agriculture, where he studied landscape design, contracting, and agriculture. Combining his education with over 25 years of work experience, Greg applies his extensive knowledge of the industry to every job that he works on. Christine is a graduate of Suffolk University with a Master's in Business Administration. Co-owner and Office Manager at Meyer Landscapes, Christine is usually the first person you will speak with when you call. With over 15 years in the Accounting and Bookkeeping industry before joining her husband at Meyer Landscapes, she brings an extra level of expertise and customer communication to the company. Greg and Christine were both born and raised on Cape Cod and have been married for nearly 15 years. They have a son together, who shares his father's passion for landscape equipment and “playing outside”. Greg and Christine have spent the last decade establishing trusted relationships with their customers and within their community.
