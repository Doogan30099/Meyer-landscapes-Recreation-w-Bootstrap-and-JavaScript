We're Hiring!

Landscape Maintenance Crew Member

Location: Harwich, MA



About Meyer Landscapes

At Meyer Landscapes, we're not just shaping outdoor spaces, we're cultivating leaders. Our mission is to grow great people and build a team of leaders. We envision being a company that maintains long-term employees by investing in leadership, accountability, and continuous improvement.



Responsibilities

  • Execute landscaping tasks such as planting, mulching, pruning, mowing, and hardscape installation.
  • Willingness to learn and take on new challenges.
  • Ability to work outdoors in various weather conditions.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
  • Prior landscaping experience is a plus but not required.


Qualifications

  • A strong work ethic and a passion for landscaping.
  • Willingness to learn and take on new challenges.
  • Ability to work outdoors in various weather conditions.
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
  • Prior landscaping experience is a plus but not required.

What Success Looks Like in 12 Months

  • Skill Mastery: You've honed your landscaping skills, from lawn mowing and plant installation to maintenance and landscape construction; demonstrating quality and efficiency.
  • Team Collaboration: You're a reliable team player, communicating effectively and supporting your colleagues.
  • Leadership Development: You've taken initiative, perhaps leading small projects or mentoring new team members.
  • Continuous Improvement: You've embraced feedback and sought opportunities to learn, reflecting our commitment to growth.

Your Impact

As a Landscape Crew Member, you'll play a vital role in bringing our clients' visions to life, enhancing the environment, and contributing to a team that values excellence and integrity. Your work will not only beautify spaces but also inspire those around you to strive for greatness.
